Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026 · App: BabyCam (com.colworx.babycam) · Developer: Zuhaib Ali Shah

In short: BabyCam turns two of your own phones into a baby monitor. The live video and audio go directly between your two devices, end-to-end encrypted — we never see or store them. We use Google Firebase for anonymous usage analytics and crash diagnostics to keep the app working. We do not sell your data, and there are no accounts.

1. Who we are

BabyCam is developed by Zuhaib Ali Shah. For any privacy question, contact mz.ashah@outlook.com.

2. Camera, microphone and audio/video streams

  • The baby device captures camera and microphone; the parent device receives them. Streaming is peer-to-peer (WebRTC) and encrypted in transit (DTLS-SRTP).
  • Audio/video is never uploaded to us or stored on any server. It exists only live, on your two devices, during a session.
  • Snapshots you choose to save are written only to your device's local gallery.

3. Pairing & connection data

  • Devices pair with a short 4-digit code shown on the baby device and typed on the parent device. Pairing information is stored only on your devices.
  • To help the two phones find each other, small connection-setup ("signaling") messages pass through a third-party MQTT message broker (HiveMQ). These messages are additionally AES-256-GCM encrypted by the app, so the broker only ever relays ciphertext. They do not contain your audio or video.
  • When the two phones are on different networks, media may be relayed through a TURN server (Cloudflare) purely to traverse firewalls. Relayed media stays end-to-end encrypted.

4. Analytics & diagnostics (Google Firebase)

To understand how the app is used and to fix crashes, we use Google Firebase, which acts as a data processor on our behalf:

ServiceWhat it collectsWhy
Firebase AnalyticsAnonymous app-usage events (e.g. pairing completed, camera toggled), device model, OS version, coarse app-interaction dataUnderstand feature usage and stability
Firebase CrashlyticsCrash stack traces, device model/OS, a random per-session identifier, and whether the device was in "baby" or "parent" modeDiagnose and fix crashes
Firebase Remote ConfigDownloads configuration only; sends no personal data Update settings (e.g. relay servers) without an app update

This website (the page you are reading) also uses Google Analytics 4 to count anonymous visits and download clicks. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, precise location, or the content of your video/audio. The 4-digit pairing code is not sent to Firebase. Firebase's own handling of this data is governed by Google's privacy terms.

6. Data retention & deletion

  • Audio/video is never retained — it is not stored anywhere.
  • Pairing data lives on your device; clearing it ("Forget pairing") or uninstalling removes it.
  • Analytics/crash data held by Firebase can be deleted on request — email mz.ashah@outlook.com and we will action deletion via Firebase.

7. Children's privacy

BabyCam is a tool for parents/guardians. It is not directed at children as users and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The camera simply serves the caregiver.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
